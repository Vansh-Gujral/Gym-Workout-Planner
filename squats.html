Squats: The King of Exercises

What Are Squats?

Squats are a compound exercise that works multiple muscle groups in the lower body, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. Squats are considered to be one of the most effective exercises for building strength and muscle in the lower body.

Benefits of Squats

  • Increase muscle mass and strength in the lower body
  • Improve athletic performance
  • Reduce the risk of injury
  • Improve balance and coordinationStrengthen the core muscles
  • Boost metabolism

How to Do a Squat

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointed slightly outward.
  2. Lower your body down as if you were sitting in a chair, keeping your back straight and your core engaged.
  3. Descend until your thighs are parallel to the ground or slightly lower.
  4. Drive through your heels to stand back up to the starting position.

Variations of Squats

There are many different variations of squats that you can do to target different muscle groups and challenge yourself.

  • Barbell squats
  • Goblet squats
  • Front squats
  • Bulgarian split squats
  • Pistol squats

Start Squatting Today!

Squats are a great exercise for people of all fitness levels. If you're new to exercise, start with a light weight and gradually increase the weight as you get stronger.
