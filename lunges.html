Lunges: The Ultimate Leg Exercise

What Are Lunges?

Lunges are a bodyweight exercise that works the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. Lunges are considered to be one of the most effective exercises for building strength and muscle in the lower body.

Benefits of Lunges

  • Increase muscle mass and strength in the lower body
  • Improve athletic performance
  • Reduce the risk of injury
  • Improve balance and coordinationStrengthen the core muscles
  • Boost metabolism

How to Do a Lunge

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Step forward with one leg and lower your body down until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle.
  3. Make sure that your front knee is directly over your ankle and that your back knee is hovering just above the ground.
  4. Drive through your front heel to push yourself back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat on the other side.

Variations of Lunges

There are many different variations of lunges that you can do to target different muscle groups and challenge yourself.

  • Walking lunges
  • Reverse lungesSide lunges
  • Bulgarian split squats
  • Dumbbell lunges
  • Barbell lunges

Start Doing Lunges Today!

Lunges are a great exercise for people of all fitness levels. If you're new to exercise, start with bodyweight lunges and gradually add weight as you get stronger.
