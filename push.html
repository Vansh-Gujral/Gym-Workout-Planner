Push-Ups: The Ultimate Bodyweight Exercise

What Are Push-Ups?

Push-ups are a bodyweight exercise that works the chest, triceps, and shoulders. Push-ups are a great exercise for building strength and muscle in the upper body.

Benefits of Push-Ups

  • Increase muscle mass and strength in the upper body
  • Improve athletic performance
  • Reduce the risk of injury
  • Improve balance and coordinationStrengthen the core muscles
  • Boost metabolism

How to Do a Push-Up

  1. Start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart and your body in a straight line from your head to your heels.
  2. Lower your body down until your chest touches the ground.
  3. Drive through your hands to push back up to the starting position.

Variations of Push-Ups

There are many different variations of push-ups that you can do to target different muscle groups and challenge yourself.

  • Incline push-ups
  • Decline push-ups
  • Wide-grip push-ups
  • Narrow-grip push-ups
  • Diamond push-ups
  • Pike push-ups

Start Doing Push-Ups Today!

Push-ups are a great exercise for people of all fitness levels. If you're new to exercise, start with your knees on the ground and gradually work your way up to full push-ups.
